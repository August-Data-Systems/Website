About


In recent years, standardized test scores have been progressively deemphasized throughout the postsecondary admissions process. This has led to questions regarding how best to judge candidates for admission, financial aid, and scholarship consideration in an automated, efficient, and equitable manner, without relying on increasingly unreliable data sources or manual processes.

At August Data Systems, we are passionate about quantifying the qualitative factors that go beyond the grades and test scores students are otherwise reduced to in the typical higher education enrollment management environment. To this end, we have developed Delta.

Delta is a Machine Learning platform designed to analyze and quantify the balance of factors specific to individual students which best predict their future success in higher education, independent of standardized test scores.

Delta allows clients to target high-potential students based on proprietary analysis of a collection of qualitative and quantitative factors, making it possible to identify, target, and more efficiently yield the right cohort of students. Delta makes it possible to eliminate traditional, expensive data purchases and targeting campaigns based upon the universally available data - standardized tests scores - that nearly all institutions yet rely on in the admissions and scholarship determination process.
