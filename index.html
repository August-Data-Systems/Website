About


Over the past years, standardized test scores have became rapidly less used throughout the admissions process. However, this has lead to questions regarding how best to judge candidates for admission, financial aid, and scholarships in an automated, efficient, and equitable manner, without relying on increasingly unreliable data sources or manual processes.

At August Data Systems, we are passionate about how we can improve the admissions process throughout higher education. Our solution to this is our signature product, Delta. Delta is a machine learning platform designed to analyze and quantify the balance of factors specific to individual students which best predict their future success in higher education, independent of standardized test scores.

Delta allows clients to target high-potential students based on its proprietary analysis, making it possible to identify, target, and more efficiently yield the right cohort of students. With Delta, you can leave the traditional, expensive targeting system centered around standardized tests scores and purchased data sets and into the enrollment system of the future.
